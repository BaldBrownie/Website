10/30-11/3 Week 11
This week we learned about electrical engineering. We talked about ohms law which is V(Volts)=I(Amps)*R(resistance). We learned about bread boards resistors and LEDs and how to make a ciruit using them. There are two types of circuits a parallel and series. A parallel the LEDs are not connected and each of them have their own resistor and connect to ground seperatly. Series the LEDs are connected and each of them have 1 resistor and 1 ground.
